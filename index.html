Che-Kai Liu

I am a senior undergraduate student at Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, Zhejiang, P.R.China. I had worked with Prof. Xunzhao Yin, Prof. X. Sharon Hu and Prof. Mohsen Imani at Zhejiang University, University of Notre Dame and UC Irvine, respectively.

Currently, my research interestes straddle architecture and autonomous applications. Before pursuing a Ph.D., research in my undergrad studies lies in compute-in-memory circuits and architecture, and hyperdimensional computing.
